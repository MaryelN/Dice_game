RULES

The game has 2 Players on the same screen

Each player have a (ROUND) and a (GLOBAL) score

Click "Roll Dice" to start the game

Each player can Roll the dice as many times as they want

Click "hold" to save your round score into the global score, and the turn will end

If the dice falls on 1 the score round score will go to 0 and the turn will end

First player to reach 50 Points wins!
